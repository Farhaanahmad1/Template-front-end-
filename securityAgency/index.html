About Bravo Security

Bravo Security Services is among the top-rated security services companies in Pakistan, providing highly trained uniformed armed and unarmed security guards, executive protection agents, and retired Special Services Group (SSG) Commandos. These professionals are trained in international (UNO) and local anti-terrorist operations and security protocol duties for high-profile Army and Civilian Executives.

Our services extend to residential, retail, commercial properties, housing societies, hospitals, schools, industries, government agencies, concerts, sporting events, political rallies, and services for overseas Pakistanis and activist demonstrations.

We also assist with neighborhood patrols, shopping malls, religious institutions, universities, gated communities, parking lots, apartment buildings, art galleries, and many more venues.
